This is Twice.

Twice are a 9 member Kpop group and part of JYP Entertainment. They were formed on the reality show SIXTEEN.

They have many number 1 hits such as I can't stop me, More & More TT and many more.

The group frequently appear on variety shows as well as having their own webseries "Time to Twice" in which they take part in tasks and have fun together.

They have many solo or smaller unit activities aswell such as melody project and photobooks ect.
